Account & Data Deletion

GospelWed respects user privacy and allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your GospelWed account, please email:

support@gospelwed.com

Use the subject line:
“Account Deletion Request – GospelWed”

In the email, include:

  • The phone number used to register on GospelWed
  • (Optional) Reason for deletion

What data will be deleted

Once your request is verified, we will delete:

  • User profile information
  • Match and interaction data
  • Uploaded photos
  • Authentication identifiers (phone number)
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data retention

Account and personal data are deleted within 30 days.

Certain minimal records may be retained temporarily for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention

Retained data is not used for any other purpose and is automatically purged.

Contact

For questions regarding data deletion, contact:
support@gospelwed.com
