Child Safety Standards

Child Safety Commitment

GospelWed is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). The safety and well-being of children are of paramount importance, and we strictly prohibit any content or behavior that endangers minors.

Age Restriction

GospelWed is strictly for users aged 18 years and older. We require age confirmation during the registration process to ensure compliance using date of birth verification.

Any account suspected to belong to a minor will be immediately suspended pending age verification. We do not knowingly collect information from or allow access to individuals under the age of 18.

Zero Tolerance Policy

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy toward:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Attempts to bypass age restrictions

Any user found engaging in such activities will face immediate account termination and a permanent ban from our platform.

Detection & Moderation

To ensure a safe environment, we employ multiple safeguards:

  • In-app Reporting: Users can easily report suspicious profiles or content directly within the app.
  • Manual Moderation: Our team reviews reported content and profiles to enforce our safety strictures.
  • Profile Screening: New profiles and content are subject to review to prevent policy violations.

Reporting & Law Enforcement Cooperation

Users are encouraged to report any child safety concerns immediately. GospelWed complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations.

We are committed to cooperating with law enforcement agencies in the investigation of crimes against children. Verified cases of CSAM will be reported to the appropriate regional and national authorities, specifically the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant local law enforcement bodies.

User Reporting Instructions

If you encounter any content or behavior that concerns you regarding child safety, please report it immediately:

  • Use the "Report" feature located on user profiles within the app.
  • Contact our safety team via email at the address below with details of the concern.
